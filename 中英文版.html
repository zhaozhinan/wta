票务条款/ Terms Conditions of Ticket Sale
1、请勿携带大包物品、饮料，严禁携带枪支、弹药、易燃易爆品和管制刀具等危险品，请自觉接受安全检查。
Large bags and beverages are not permitted in the WTA Finals Shenzhen. Guns/firearms and ammunition/explosive goods are strictly prohibited into the stadium.
2、每人一票，比赛前一小时开始验票入场，请配合接受安检。
Ticket check points will open one(1) hour before the start of the first match. Please accept security check.
3、比赛期间请将手机置于静音或震动模式，并禁止使用闪光灯。
All mobile phones must be switched to Silent or Vibrate on Silent. Flash is prohibited.
4、比赛期间，严禁在赛场内任何位置吸烟。
Smoking is prohibited in any place of stadium.
5、比赛期间禁止大声喧哗，在选手交换场地时，才能起身走动或离开座位。 Please be quiet during match play and do not leave the seats until changeovers.
6、门票为实名售票，一旦出售，概不退票退款。观众必须持当日当场次的有效票进场，每票一人一座，无副券则作废。
Once ticket is sold, no refund will be offered. All spectators must have a valid ticket to enter the stadium, one ticket per person and no entry is allowed without the ticket stub.
7、为了提高您和他人的观赛体验以及共同维护良好且安静的比赛环境，3岁以下儿童不建议入场，3岁以上儿童凭票入场。
Children under 3 years of age are not suggested into the stadium.
8、在未得到2019深圳WTA年终总决赛组委会书面许可的情况下，持票者不得将赛票转卖、转赠或者用作商业推广用途，一经发现，没收赛票不予退款。
Tickets shall not be resold and/or used for any other promotional campaigns without the prior written approval of the 2019 WTA Finals Shenzhen Organizing Committee.
9、持票人员不得以任何形式或者目的在任何一场比赛期间（从比赛开始到比赛结束）持续收集、宣传、传播、出版或发布任何比分或相关的统计数据用于商业或者赌博用途。禁止在赛事比赛场地范围内（观众区）持续使用笔记本电脑或其他手持电子设备。除非是持证媒体人员、赛事供应商及赛事工作人员在履行其职责。违者将追究其法律责任。
No ticket holder or spectator may continually collect, disseminate, transmit, publish or release from the grounds of Tournament any match scores or related statistical data during match play (from the commencement of a match through its conclusion for any commercial, betting or gambling purpose).The continual use of laptop computers or other handheld electronic devices within the confines(spectator area)of the tournament match courts is prohibited. The exception to this provision is properly credentialed media, tournament vendors and tournament staff when used in the performance of their duties.
10、本政策内容由2019深圳WTA年终总决赛组委会发布，对于本政策内容约定不明或因释义上产生分歧时，由深圳国际仲裁院裁决。
In case of dispute, 2019 WTA Finals Shenzhen Organizing Committee’s decision shall be final.
